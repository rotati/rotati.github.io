ABOUT

Cambosearch is a Technology Search Engine in Cambodia. It contains technology Companys, Freelancers, and NGOs in Cambodia.

Cambosearch contains details about the size, strength, portfolio and contact details of many of the top technology service providers in Cambodia. For convenience, the service providers are grouped by their service offerings (e.g. web application development, mobile app development, content writing, online marketing, search engine optimization etc) and are all clearly indicated if they provide training services.

CAMBOSEARCH STORY

Cambosearch was developed by Rotati Consultant after Development Innovation (USAID) doing a research and identified that CSOs and NGOs in Cambodia found it difficult to find the right technology service provider to solve their technology problems and design solutions with the skills they need and their the contact information.

Cambosearch project was built by using Agile Methodology which means we thought of Customer satisfaction by early, Welcome changing requirements, Delivered software frequentyl (week by week), Fact-to-face conversation etc.

On 22nd October 2015, Rotati CEO, Darren Jensen met and demoed Cambosearch to the US Ambassador to Cambodia!

On 05th November 2015, Rotati CEO, Darren Jensen with Development Innovation organized a launching event of CamboSearch. Darren Jensen presented details about the motivation behind CamboSearch and where it is heading in the future. He also showed how to search on CamboSearch and how to register their technology services on this new Cambodian directory.
